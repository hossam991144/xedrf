Geographic Information Systems

 
  By/Hossam Mohamed


        *Geographic Information Systems (GIS) is a field that combines technology
        and geography to collect, store, analyze, and display data about specific
        locations on the Earth's surface, This field relies on digital maps and
        spatial data to understand geographic relationships and patterns.
